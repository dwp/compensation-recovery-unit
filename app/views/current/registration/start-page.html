Register a compensation recovery claim

Use this service to:

  • register a new road traffic accident claim
  • view an existing road traffic accident claim to check what you may owe
  • settle a road traffic accident claim

Compensators who receive a claim for compensation must register the claim within 14 days.

Registering the claim does not mean that you have accepted liability for the accident or injury.

Registering takes around 5 minutes.

To view or settle an existing claim you'll need:

  • the claim reference number
  • the injured person's date of birth
This service is only available to pre-registered organisations while we test how it works. You'll need the username and password you were given to sign in.

Before you start

You can also register by post.

You can only use this service to register road traffic accident claims (also known as motor liability). To register any other claim including public liability, employer liability and clinical negligence, you must complete a CRU1 form instead.

You cannot use this service unless you were given a username and password.
